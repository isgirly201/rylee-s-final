Education

I graduated from MVHS in May of 2021. I am grateful for the experience I had to grow and learn the basics in high school. After high school, I served a mission in San Bernardino, CA wehre I invested a lot of time into growing spiritually and intellectually. I couldn't be more grateful for my mission.

An important thing to point out is that from August until December 2021 I did one semester online at ASU. I had always dreamed of living in Hawaii so that is what I did. With the help of my parents and the Lord I was able to fulfill this dream. I finished some generals and did my best to manage having fun and going to school. During my time in Hawaii, I learned of a program called EXDM. I became so obsessed with that program that I decided to apply to BYU since it is the only school to offer that program.

After returning home from my mission, I transfered to BYU and started the journey of preparing to apply to EXDM. I have done all that I could to get as much experience as possible and to make myself a good candidate for the program. I will apply this coming May for the program and hope to start the program in the Fall of 2024!
